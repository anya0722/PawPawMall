About Us.

How we began:

PawPawMall started in a small apartment with one hungry kitten and a mission to find the healthiest, most engaging products on the market. We realized that big-box stores often missed the soul and safety that picky cat parents demand.

PawPaw Mall Concept:

Every item in our store is "Paw-picked." This means it undergoes a rigorous testing phase by our feline team and a nutritional review by experts. If it doesn't inspire a purr or a zoomie, it doesn't make the cut.
